About the ACT

The ACT is a standardized test used for college admissions in the United States. It measures skills in English, Math, Reading, Science, and Writing.

Why Take the ACT?

College Admissions

Boost your chances of getting into top universities with a competitive ACT score.

Scholarships

High ACT scores can help you qualify for scholarships and reduce college costs.

Career Readiness

Understand and showcase your strengths in critical thinking and problem-solving.
