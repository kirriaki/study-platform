About TOEFL

The TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) is a standardized test to measure the English language proficiency of non-native speakers. It is widely accepted by universities and institutions around the world.

TOEFL scores are used for admission to English-speaking universities and colleges, scholarship programs, and visa applications. A good TOEFL score can enhance your academic and professional opportunities globally.

Registration Information

To register for the TOEFL exam, follow these steps:

  1. Create an account on the official TOEFL website.
  2. Select a test date and location that suits you.
  3. Fill in the required personal and contact information.
  4. Pay the exam fee using a valid payment method.
  5. Confirm your registration and receive your admission ticket.

The registration fee for the TOEFL exam varies by country. Please check the official TOEFL website for the exact fee in your region.

TOEFL test centers are available worldwide. You can find the nearest test center using the official TOEFL test center locator tool on their website.
